Website Development

Professional, secure, and responsive websites tailored to your business needs

Overview

At Fortishield-Matrix, we design and develop websites that combine security, speed, and scalability. Each site is tailored to your business requirements, from simple informational sites to complex interactive platforms. We prioritize user experience, mobile responsiveness, and SEO performance while integrating robust security protocols to protect your data.

Types of Websites

Static Websites

Ideal for companies that require an online presence with fixed content. Fast, secure, and low-maintenance, these sites provide essential information and branding without backend complexity.

  • Fast loading times
  • Enhanced security with no database vulnerabilities
  • Lower hosting costs
  • Perfect for portfolios, brochures, and small business sites

Dynamic Websites

Database-driven websites that allow content updates, user interactions, and e-commerce capabilities. Perfect for blogs, portals, or enterprise applications requiring continuous content management.

  • Content Management System (CMS) integration
  • User authentication and profiles
  • Interactive features and forms
  • Database-driven content

E-Commerce Platforms

Fully integrated online stores with secure payment gateways, product management, and analytics. Designed to optimize user experience, conversion rates, and operational efficiency.

  • Secure payment processing
  • Inventory management system
  • Customer account management
  • Order tracking and analytics

Development Methodologies

  • Agile Development: Rapid iterations with continuous feedback to meet your evolving business needs.
  • Responsive Design: Optimized for all devices, ensuring smooth navigation on desktops, tablets, and smartphones.
  • SEO Optimization: Enhanced search engine visibility through technical and content strategies.
  • Security Best Practices: Implementation of HTTPS, firewalls, and data encryption to protect user information.
  • Performance Optimization: Fast load times, optimized assets, and server configurations for seamless user experience.

Website Maintenance & Hosting Services

Domain Registration

We assist you in registering your preferred domain name. You may choose your domain registrar, or we can recommend reliable options:

  • Namecheap
  • GoDaddy
  • Google Domains
  • Bluehost
  • Hostinger

Domain registration cost is separate and depends on the registrar and domain type (.com, .net, .co, etc.).

Web Hosting

We support all major hosting providers. Clients can choose their preferred hosting agent:

  • Shared Hosting: Affordable, suitable for small websites.
  • VPS Hosting: More control, dedicated resources.
  • Cloud Hosting: Scalable and flexible for growing businesses.
  • Dedicated Hosting: Full server for large websites with high traffic.

We can assist with setup, migration, and optimization on the chosen platform. Hosting fees are billed by the provider and are not included in our service package.

Maintenance & Support

We offer optional maintenance plans to keep your website secure, fast, and up-to-date:

  • Regular updates (CMS, plugins, frameworks)
  • Security monitoring and patches
  • Performance optimization
  • Daily or weekly backups
  • Bug fixes and technical support

Maintenance fees are monthly or annual and can be tailored to your requirements.
